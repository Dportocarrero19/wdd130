Rivers

Jordan Rapids

The Jordan rapids, located in West Virginia, is a difficult course having many narrow rapids. This course is not intended for beginners and tests, even those who are greatly skilled. You should have some experience in rafting before attempting this course. Be prepared for a bumpy ride!

Whitefall Canyon

The whitefall canyon river, located in Colorado, is a very scenic journey. There are no rapids on this course, so you are able to take out your camera to take some memorable photos. Enjoy the vast scenery produced by these huge mountains.

George River

The George River, located in Washington, is a great beginner course for introducing your friends rafting. There are few rapids for helping begginers learn the ropes but nothing major to knock you off the boat. All ages are encouraged to try this river.

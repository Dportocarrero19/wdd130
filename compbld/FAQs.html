FAQs

How much is a PC system, and do I have to build my own?

PC price can depend on what you want to use it for. A PC used for browning the web and managing a few applications can be in the mid hundreds, while gaming computers can reach thousands. You do not have to build your own, as there are some websites that allow you to pre-build your computer by selecting parts for them to build and ship your system.

How long does it take to build a PC?

Depending on your level of experience, it can take around 3-5 hours to build your computer system. I would recommend doing extensive research when building a computer with expensive parts.

What do I do if my computer does not turn on?

If your computer does not turn on, there could be many different kinds of problems.First, rule out any common mistakes. Make sure each component is secured and plugged in correctly. If everything is in order, there could be a damaged part which could have been shipped. Check with the computer manufacturer.
